Recommendations
Quick win (do it in Synvya) Requires action outside Synvya
  • 1
    Publish your menu as structured data. AI assistants cannot read PDF menus. Connect your Square POS in the Menu tab to automatically publish menu items with names, prices, and descriptions that AI can parse. This single action impacts 60% of your score.
  • 2
    Add dietary tags to your menu items. When someone asks "restaurants with vegan options near me," AI assistants need explicit dietary tags — not just mentions in descriptions. Add tags in the Menu tab.
  • 3
    Re-publish your discovery page. After adding menu data, re-publish from Settings to update your Schema.org JSON-LD with Menu, MenuItem, prices, and dietary info — the format AI engines trust most.
  • 4
    Set your business category on Google. Log into Google Business Profile and set your primary category (e.g., "Mexican Restaurant"). AI assistants use this to match cuisine-specific queries.
  • 5
    List your business on Yelp and delivery platforms. AI assistants cross-reference Google, Yelp, DoorDash, and Uber Eats. Ensure your menu and hours are consistent across all platforms where you're listed.
